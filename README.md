## Quantitative Finance Projects

1. **Vanilla Options Pricing (Black–Scholes)**  
   Analytical pricing, error analysis.

2. **Vanilla Greeks – Analytical & Monte Carlo**  
   Pathwise & likelihood ratio Delta estimation

3. **Hedging in a Black–Scholes World**  
   Delta, Gamma, Stop-Loss strategies; variance vs. rebalancing frequency, drift, volatility; time-dependent vol hedging.

4. **Recombining Trees – Binomial & Trinomial**  
   European/American vanilla & barrier options; early exercise; convergence to BSM.

5. **Exotic Options by Monte Carlo**  
   Asian & continuous barrier options; convergence vs. monitoring frequency, maturity, barrier level.

6. **Low-Discrepancy Sequences**  
   Sobol sequences + Cholesky/Spectral decomposition; significant MC convergence speedup.

7. **Static Replication of Continuous Barrier Options**  
   Portfolio replication method; convergence vs. discretization; time-dependent volatility extensions.

8. **Multi-Asset Options – Quantos & Margrabe**  
   Analytical & MC pricing; convergence analysis of correlated-asset Monte Carlo.

9. **Simple Interest-Rate Derivatives**  
   FRAs, caplets/floorlets, European swaptions; analytical pricing.

10. **LIBOR-in-Arrears Convexity**  
    Analytical approximation, full Monte Carlo, numerical integration pricer; method comparison.

11. **American Options – Replication & Binomial Trees**  
    Early-exercise pricing via static replication & recombining trees; convergence vs. steps.

12. **LIBOR Market Model (Brace - Gatarek - Musiela model)**  
    Multi-factor forward LIBOR simulation; caplet/swaption pricing; numeraire invariance checks.

13. **Stochastic & Rough Volatility Models**  
    Heston & Rough Heston (Quadratic Exponential discretization); analytical sanity checks (char. function, variance expectation); exotic pricing; realistic ATM skew generation (power-law behavior matching empirical data).
